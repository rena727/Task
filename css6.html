Welcome To Eco Harvest

«ECO HARVEST» specializes in supplies of fresh natural fruits, berries, vegetables and
greenery from Turkey, Azerbaijan and Kyrgyzstan to CIS and other countries. Our
products are ecologically clean, grown in natural environment without usage of any
chemicals and mineral fertilizers, due to which our fruits, berries and vegetables have
specific natural taste. We have proven ourselves as a reliable supplier of high quality
fresh natural products from Turkey, Azerbaijan and Kyrgyzstan. One of our company’s
main principles is to ensure high quality of products we offer. We supply selected fruits,
berries, vegetables and greenery, which pass careful calibration and quality inspection
